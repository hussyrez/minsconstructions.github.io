Rendering FAQ

Common questions about render and texture services in Melbourne

Cement rendering uses a sand and cement mix for a strong, traditional finish ideal for brick and block walls. Acrylic rendering uses a polymer-modified mix that is more flexible, crack-resistant, and comes pre-mixed in a wide range of colours. Both are popular choices for Melbourne homes.

Rendering in Melbourne typically costs $30 to $60 per square metre depending on the type of render, wall condition, and project size. Cement rendering is at the lower end while acrylic and texture coatings are at the higher end. Contact us for a free on-site quote.

A professionally applied cement render lasts 20 to 30 years, while acrylic rendering typically lasts 15 to 25 years. Texture coatings offer similar longevity. Proper surface preparation and quality materials are the key factors in render durability.
