Architectural Mouldings for Melbourne Homes

Decorative mouldings add character, elegance, and street appeal to any property. At Mins Constructions, we supply and install a wide range of architectural mouldings that transform plain facades into distinctive, eye-catching exteriors. From classic window surrounds to modern geometric profiles, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

Our mouldings are crafted from lightweight EPS (expanded polystyrene) and coated with a durable polymer-modified render finish. They look identical to traditional concrete or plaster mouldings but weigh a fraction as much, making them faster to install, more cost-effective, and resistant to cracking.

Our Moulding Services

  • Window & Door Surrounds — Framing that adds definition and style to openings, available in a range of profiles from simple to ornate.
  • Cornices & Band Mouldings — Horizontal mouldings that run along rooflines or between storeys for architectural detail.
  • Columns & Pilasters — Decorative columns and flat pilasters that add grandeur and structure to entrances and facades.
  • Keystones & Quoins — Accent pieces for window tops, corners, and arches that add a classic finish.
  • Custom Profiles — Bespoke moulding designs manufactured to your specifications for a truly unique look.

Why Choose Our Moulding Services

Lightweight & Durable

EPS mouldings are a fraction of the weight of concrete, reducing structural load while providing the same visual impact. The render-coated finish is weather-resistant and built to last in Melbourne conditions.

Seamless Integration

Our team installs and renders mouldings to blend seamlessly with your existing walls. The finished result looks like it was built into the original structure, not added later.

Custom Design Options

From traditional heritage profiles to contemporary geometric shapes, we can source or manufacture mouldings to match your exact design requirements and architectural style.
