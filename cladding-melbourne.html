External Cladding Solutions for Melbourne

External cladding protects your building from the elements while creating a clean, modern facade. At Mins Constructions, we install and render a range of cladding systems designed to withstand Melbourne's demanding weather conditions while giving your property a seamless, contemporary finish.

Whether you are cladding a new build, replacing damaged cladding, or rendering over existing panels, our licenced team delivers precise installation and quality rendered finishes that last. We work with builders, homeowners, and commercial property owners across Melbourne and South East suburbs.

Our Cladding Services

  • Hebel Panel Cladding — Lightweight autoclaved aerated concrete panels with excellent thermal and acoustic insulation, rendered to a smooth finish.
  • EPS Cladding Systems — Expanded polystyrene cladding for insulation and rendering, ideal for energy-efficient building envelopes.
  • Render Over Cladding — Applying a seamless render finish over existing cladding materials for a uniform, weather-resistant exterior.
  • Fibre Cement Cladding — Durable fibre cement sheets installed and finished to create a clean, modern look.
  • Cladding Repairs & Replacement — Removing damaged or non-compliant cladding and replacing with safe, rendered alternatives.

Benefits of External Cladding

Weather Protection

Cladding creates an additional barrier against rain, wind, UV exposure, and temperature extremes. A properly installed and rendered cladding system keeps your building protected year-round.

Thermal Insulation

Hebel and EPS cladding systems provide excellent thermal insulation, reducing heat loss in winter and heat gain in summer. This can lower your energy bills and improve indoor comfort.

Modern Appearance

Cladding gives your property a clean, contemporary facade. When rendered to a smooth finish, it conceals structural elements and creates a seamless exterior that boosts street appeal and property value.
