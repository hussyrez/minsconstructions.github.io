Frequently Asked Questions About Rendering

Common questions about our Melbourne rendering services

Rendering costs in Melbourne typically range from $30 to $60 per square metre depending on the type of render (cement or acrylic), surface condition, and project size. Mins Constructions offers free on-site quotes so you get an accurate price for your specific project.

We offer a full range of rendering services across Melbourne including cement rendering, acrylic rendering, texture coating, Hebel rendering, float and set plastering, concrete rendering, wall rendering, render cladding, and sand and cement repairs for both residential and commercial properties.

A standard house rendering project in Melbourne typically takes 3 to 7 days depending on the size of the property, the type of render chosen, and weather conditions. Larger commercial projects may take longer. We provide a clear timeline with every quote.

Cement rendering uses a sand and cement mix that creates a strong, traditional finish ideal for brick and block walls. Acrylic rendering uses a polymer-modified mix that is more flexible, crack-resistant, and comes in a wider range of colours and textures. Both are popular choices for Melbourne homes.
